The Problem

Marketing is broken
for most businesses.

The average SME stitches together 6–10 tools and still can't answer "is our marketing working?" at the end of the month.

01
Brand goes off-script every week
Different tones, wrong colours, inconsistent messaging — because there's no single source of truth that everyone follows.
02
Content approval is a WhatsApp thread
Posts go live without the right eyes on them. Mistakes reach thousands before anyone notices. No audit trail.
03
6 tools, 6 logins, 6 monthly fees
Canva. Buffer. Hootsuite. Spreadsheet for reporting. Email for briefs. None of them talk to each other.
04
ROI is always "unclear"
Spend is visible. Returns aren't. Because the data lives in five different platforms that no one has time to reconcile.
